    220 YEARS SERGIUS ZHIVAGO

              

              28/12/2014. 20:53.October 4 marks 220 years since the birth of the entrepreneur, social activist, philanthropist, founder of the Ryazan city public bank Sergei Afanasevicha Zhivago (1794-1866), a native of the city of Ryazan. Of course, since our site earned later, we publish about it just now, but in fact our museum is closely connected with this person, not by chance that he called "the history of the development of entrepreneurship charity and the Ryazan region. "Many people know Sergius Zhivago as the founder of the bank, which still exists today. So, in honor of this momentous event, the activists held a tour of the museum and created a section of the exhibition. Today's entrepreneurs can learn from philanthropists Ryazan land XIX century: not only do they have multiplied their capital, but also did a lot for the poor, offended and disadvantaged people. Thus, the bank provided Zhivago neschastnorozhdennyh children social system Support from birth to exit into adulthood.
