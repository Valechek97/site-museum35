Kolesnik was the deputy of the Ryazan Regional Duma second, third and fourth convocations, Chairman of the Committee on the State, local government, the rule of law, Law Enforcement and Public Relations Associations Regional Duma of the fourth convocation. Kolesnik was for many years the head of the Ryazan Automobile Sports Federation, a member of the party "United Russia».

               Position: Deputy Ryazan Regional Duma, General Director of JSC "Russian Leather».

    He worked as a service engineer, chief of the Operations Department, Deputy General Director of JSC "RPAP-2" (Ryazan taxi station).

    VOLODIN ALEXSEI MIHAILOVICH

               Position: General Director of JSC "Tyazhpressmash." Date of Birth: 03/27/1947 Education: After graduating from Penza Polytechnic Institute and military service with the post master control TCI began its development as a professional. Biographical information: His track record of a number of senior positions - foreman, deputy chief engineer, Deputy General Director for Production, Chief Engineer.
               Under his leadership and with his direct participation have been developed and the development of a wide range of unique, powerful, sophisticated equipment - are automated horizontal forging machines, radial forging machines, forging manipulators, hydraulic and mechanical presses, automatic production lines and complexes. The company has gained a strong reputation for best in the industry. Modern high-forging machine Ryazan brand supplied to both domestic and foreign customers. For great merits in professional activities in 1996. AM Volodin was awarded the honorary title "Honored Engineer of the Russian Federation" .He is the author of a number of inventions to improve the manufacturing processes, equipment and tools. For success in this work, in 1999 he was awarded the State Prize of the Russian Federation in the field of science and technology. In 1994, as Director General of JSC "Tyazhpressmash" he resolutely set about reforming the structure of production, took a tough, but the only right decision - to seek new markets, move to the release of new products. And only thanks to his initiative, his personal creative contribution, his perseverance and confidence in the success of the factory workers (in collaboration with the scientific and technical centers of oil and gas), were able to learn and start producing products for the oil and gas industry. Alexis - a talented engineer, under his direction a large-scale reorganization of the metallurgical division of "Tyazhpressmash." Company: JSC "Tyazhpressmash" Main activity: Forging equipment, metallurgical industry, oil and gas equipment for the conservation and wells. Website: http://www.tkpo.ryazan.ru/
