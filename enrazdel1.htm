    CLOTHING RYAZAN PROVINCE

               Various types of clothing first half of XX century are presented Klepikovsky County. The shape and cut it were the same for all classes. Clothing differed only on one used by the material. It was the custom to put on top of the bottom a few shirts. We see an expensive men's long-sleeved shirt of silk fabrics on a paper-lined, decorated with folds, ruffles, and a beautiful dress of a peasant girl.
               Underwear was spacious with long sleeves. Sewed her "in a row", ie without lining. In peasant families were the main footwear sandals.

         

    RAG-DOLLS

    HANDUCRAFT PRODUCTION

               Small-scale craft production in the late XIX - early XX century was one of the most important aspects of the economic life of the Russian people. From early childhood, among the peasants tried every obzatelno grasp the basics of all the necessary activities and crafts. The main agricultural work starts with the melting of snow and end by Cope 1 (14) of October. After that, the women were engaged in perfecting flax, crushed, Czeslaw tow, spun, dried skin. Gras began to weave, this stopping work to sowing days. Men cut wood, carried firewood, doing crafts or went into town on seasonal work, going back to the beginning of plowing.
