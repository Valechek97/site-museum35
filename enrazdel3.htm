    PETR ALEXSEEVICH MALSHIN

               In autumn 2007, sublimely solemn, light and joy were held in Ryazan during the celebration of the 200th anniversary of Skorbjashchenskoj church. This is the only temple of God in Ryazan, which was not closed any hard times of persecution of the church, nor in war and postwar years. The rest were subjected to desecration and destruction, and many no trace.
               At the same time celebrated the 255th anniversary of Peter I Malshina, by means of which was built this temple. How can you not surprised? Truly - it is not an accidental coincidence of anniversaries, and fishing Almighty?
               Now appropriate to recall the well-known philanthropist Ryazan again. Because 200 years ago opened the first hospice in our city (1809) which was built and kept the same at his expense.
               Beneficence PA Malshina served for the benefit of the Ryazan hramostroitelstva, education, cultural development, social ... An important part of the activities of this extraordinary personality was also the city's public and the public service.
               Let's start with the history of the race Malshinyh, which goes back to ancient times.
               In the years 1595-1597 Daniel Malshin had shelves (counter) in the meat row Lower tenements, and later traded in his barn in the city (ie the territory of modern Ryazan Kremlin). His son, Kuzma Malshin possessing entrepreneurial talent, not only continued his father's work, but also made significant progress in trade.
               By the early seventeenth century Malshiny mentioned in the discharge and payment books Pereyaslavl-Ryazan peasants Borisoglebskaya settlement belonging to the bishop. (Now here Seminary Street).
               Tsar Alexei Mikhailovich, taking care of the replenishment of the treasury, in 1649 mandated that "the best in their trades and crafts" peasants in black townsmen settlement. These number of hit and Kuzma Malshin transferred from Borisoglebskaya settlement, do not pay taxes. So Malshiny steel tradespeople people New Chernoposadskoy settlement (now the streets are Catholic and Postal).
               We grandson Kuzma Kuchma, Epiphanes Solzhenitsyn, had four sons: Nazar, Ivan, Peter and Matthew. But only the descendants of the eldest of them went in for trade. So, Nazar Epifanovich be called the founder of the dynasty of merchants, for his sons - Alex and Lukyan - in the middle of the eighteenth century were considered already Ryazan merchants.
               The family Nazarovich Alexei and his wife Mary Tikhonovna Malshinyh in 1752 had a son, Peter, who became one of the most famous philanthropists among Ryazan merchants.
               Make a fortune and secure social position Peter A. Malshin could, occupy a favorable while the mercy of wine. In 1783-1787 years he, together with Gavrila Vasilyevich Rumin, filmed wine farming in the city of Ryazan, Mikhailov and the Saviour with the counties.
               It was the largest in Ryazan salotopenny plant, which annually produces up to 40 pounds of fat. When the plant is working picker. Fat and hemp went to the capital.
               Things went well and in 1790 its capital exceeded 50 thousand rubles. Peter Malshin became a merchant 1st guild and received a distinguished citizen.
               In the years 1791- 1794 Peter A. was mayor. As stated in the certificate issued to him Ryazan policeman magistrate service he performed his "good faith and without burdening society, repairing the full benefits grad benefits».
               In the early nineteenth century, Peter A. Malshin was the largest landlord in Ryazan. He owned 10 houses, most of which were profitable, ie surrendered vnaёm for residential, commercial and warehouse space.
               Malshinu brought considerable income and estates with serfs in the village of Ryazan province Ablova and Saransk County Penza province. By the way, the right to buy the peasants he bought together with the rank of court counselor, granted him by the decree of Paul-I in 1800.
               He received a knighthood and joined the civil service, was eliminated from the official merchants. But continued to engage in business. Peter A. in 1800-1810-ies took contracts for the supply of salt in Ryazan, Tula and Tambov Province.
               Peter A. Malshin earned the respect of citizens for their charity, which is actively engaged with the 90-ies of the XVIII century. The first charity shares Malshin carried out in conjunction with GV Rumin. In 1793, the construction of a building for the General People's College, they donated five thousand rubles. Together, they have financed the construction of the first tier and the cathedral bell tower.
               In the years 1801-1804, Peter Malshin participated in the rescue of the cathedral of the Assumption Cathedral from destruction. Contrary to the decision of the Synod of its demolition, they GV Rumin initiated the restoration of the shrine, took over the organization of all preparatory and restoration work and paid a significant portion of the cost (twenty thousand fifty). Of these twenty - malshinskih had fourteen thousand.
               The second main temple Ryazan - winter Nativity Cathedral (former Assumption) - was also blessed with Peter Alexeyevich. In the last year of his life he gave there are two icons in silver-gilt vestments: the temple icon of the Nativity and the icon of St. Nicholas. Helped Peter A. and Sarov.
               But mostly the brainchild of philanthropist was open in 1809 hospice. In 1806, PA Malshin made fifty thousand rubles in the Order of Public Welfare the establishment of a nursing home. Fifteen thousand of them were used for the construction of a stone building, and thirty-five thousand rubles - leaving them "Forever untouchable capital", the interest from which is intended for the maintenance of Ryazan men's almshouse for the elderly 12.
               This event had great resonance in society. A special decree of June 5, 1806, addressed to Peter Alekseevich Malshinu Alexander-I expressed my "Special grace" to act Malshina. The same decree founding almshouses "graciously granted" was the Order of Anna 2 nd degree.
               Construction of facilities was completed in December 1808, and in 1809 a hospice was opened.
               Originally it contained 12 retired soldiers and 12 poor burghers and peasants government. Until 1816 contained a merciful institution entirely on funds bequeathed Malshinym. When the number of its inhabitants has increased to 34 people, some of the costs assumed the Order of public charity.
               In 1832, Emperor Nicholas-I, being in Ryazan, visited the poorhouse, found her "in good form" and deemed it necessary to continue to refer to the institution "Disability Malshina home", as most prizrevaemyh it were retired soldiers.
               In 1880, there were held more than 100 men and women.

In 1807, at the newly formed Skorbjashchenskom cemetery it means stone church was built, which houses three altars: the main - in honor of the Mother of God "All the Afflicted", Right - in honor of Alexis righteous man of God and the left - in honor of St. Onuphrius the Great.
