    HISTORY MUSEUM

               Every touch of the past - it is not just knowledge, it is the emotions, feelings, emotions, not allowing us to become "Ivanov, not remembering kinship". Therefore it is difficult to overestimate the role of school museums designed to educate the children a sense of homeland, a sense of gratitude and appreciation to the people inscribed in the annals of our country's brightest pages.

               History of the Museum School №35 began with lessons in the local history club "Native Land". The work was writing the book "Meschera" and the collection objects of museum value. Thus was born the idea of creating a museum dedicated to the history of philanthropy and entrepreneurship in the Ryazan region.

    Organizer and head of the museum - a teacher of history and social Galina Ivanova. Creating the city's first museum of its kind, it clearly caught the pulse of the times. Galina - Excellence in technical and vocational Education of the Russian Federation, the winner of the best teachers in the National Priority Project "Education", winner of the municipal stage of the All-Russian contest "Teacher of the Year of Russia". Galina continues teaching a kind of dynasty, of which more than 300 years.

              The teacher with the children went to the villages Kasimovsky, Klepikovsky District Ryazan region, bit by bit to gather materials for exposure. As a truly enthusiastic teacher, has created around himself a team of pupils who are interested in the work of the museum. Thanks to her enthusiasm and great diligence it was opened in 2005

              Black-and-white photographs and objects tell about exposure Ryazan province in the XIX-XX centuries. Experience the atmosphere of a past life, listen to the voices of past generations to help windows, which represent the old money, authentic items retail, scores, the book accounting of goods in 1897. The exhibition introduces the Ryazan philanthropist and benefactor PA Malshinym, GV Rumin, SA Zhivago. section "The history of industrial production" booklets about modern enterprises of the region, design and research work of children, presentations, archival documents.

              The most beloved and interesting for students in the school excursion is "history of the village Kanishchevo." Here are the objects of traditional folk crafts and trades. The museum has interactive Fund, which allows touch antique household items: tubs, pails, buckets, shoes, and tuesok box-scrip. Vintage pots, Krynki, bowls, dishes, pots help clearly present the country life. Handicraft items remind that every woman in the peasant family could sew, weave, knit, embroider, weave lace. The history of the village Kanishchevo - a piece of homeland, the place where the home, school, PAU live friends, so the children are so fond of this tour.

              Every year replenished the main and auxiliary funds. Now we are working on the creation of a peasant's hut interior exposure.

              The direction of activity of the museum "History of the development of entrepreneurship and philanthropy Ryazan region" deal: search and collecting work, scientific research search, conducting excursions, the publication of articles and teaching materials.

              Museum - Center of military-patriotic education. Frequent visitors to the school - war and labor veterans, war, international, noble countrymen. Became traditional event "these days will never fade glory" "Children of War"; quiz "Do you know your city?", "Do you know his countrymen -geroev Great Patriotic War?"; Laying flowers at the memorial plaques of famous people Kanishchevo neighborhood.

              The book reviews many entries guests, parents of schoolchildren about their impressions, which were obtained after a visit to the museum. The 65th anniversary of Victory in the Great Patriotic War held temticheskie interesting excursions. Pupils moving, with tears in his eyes told of the front and the everyday life of their relatives during the Great Patriotic War. Do not forget the names of graduates who honorably fulfilled their internationalist duty in Chechnya and Afghanistan.

               Among these graduates - Zorkin Igor . Private, bomber. Born April 13, 1964 in the city of Ryazan. He graduated from vocational school №11 in 1862 in his hometown. He worked as a mechanic at the combine plant. In the armed forces is designed in April 1983 Moscow RVC Ryazan. In the Republic of Afghanistan from July 1983. heavily ill September 27th of the same year he died. Igor Zorkin buried at the cemetery Novogorazhdanskom Ryazan.

              Another graduate - Firsov Andrew Y. . Born June 1, 1985, a graduate of economy class in 2002. Purposeful person. Selecting one future profession, everything is subject to the achievement of their dreams. Andrei had many friends, Life, humorist. He is always with all excited. With great respect for the teachers, parents, the older generation. He graduated from Ryazan branch of the University with honors from the Ministry of Interior. Became a lieutenant of militia. He served in the Office for Combating Organized Crime. Killed in the line of duty in the republic of Ingushetia. He was posthumously awarded the Order of Courage decree President of the Russian Federation dated April 10, 2004.

               Brings many benefits to children regional studies: it not only expands the horizons of knowledge and enrich social experience, but also deepens their spiritual world. school museum contributes to the great cause of conservation the memory of the glorious people of Ryazan Region and their noble work. Good lessons remind us of the true high for human life on earth - the service of God, the people and the fatherland
